Origins

Physical art dates back as far as humans do from cave paintings to king tuts tomb to statues of greek gods all the way to present day. Traditional/physical art is the most popular form of art mostly because its how digital artists usually get started.

Types

Physical art can be almost anything. Jewlry making, painting, sculpting and sewing are all huge portions of physical art but those are simply umbrellas for the many many more forms of art that could be considered those things.

What Is It

Physical art or a traditional art is the process of creating art with physical objecs like pen and paper instead of using graphics and pixles like digital art.
