What this website is going to be about in general is art if you couldnt guess hat already. But more specifically types of art and what they entail. First we will have different types of art, mostly general ones that most people have encountered in their life. Then we will look at digital and physical art separately. Keep in mind, I wont be taking about music, dance, or performance art. Mostly sculpting, animation, painting, drawing Etc Etc.
