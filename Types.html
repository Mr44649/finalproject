Types of Art
Catagories Medium About
Traditional
(Usually handheld art product, like a pencil)		 Pencil and Paper This type of art is done with paper and pencil. This is probably the first or one of the first types of art you think of. It's usually a starting point for most artists. It is one of the most accesable and cheap ways to do art.
Charcoal Charcoal can be done with may different types of charcoal, like compressed charcoal which has a chalky feeling, or vine charcoal wich is so light that is almost feels like nothing.
Pen/Ink Pen and ink is usually done with things like dip pens and india ink or with ballpoint pens. Those items are all considered pen or ink products.
Calligraphy Calligraphy is a subtype of pen and ink. It's usually done with a dip pen or fountain pen but can also be done with a simple paintbrush. It's the art of lettering and most times is in cursive.
Colored Pencil Just like graphite pencils, colored pencils are mostly used on paper but instead of grey's it's colors.
Paint
(Any type of medium
considered paint)		 Acrylic A type of paint that is most commonly used. It dries more like rubber and is usually very pigmented
Tempra Also commonly used, tempra paint is water based and very translucent. It will not stain fabric like other paints and is common for children to use.
Watercolor A pigment that is usually in a pallate or tube. Water is used to water the pigment down. Watercolor is easily layered and a flowing paint unlike acrylic.
Oil Paint Another common one, oil pain is like acrylics transparency wise, but it is oil based. It takes longer to dry and was extremely common in paintings.
Gouache Gouache is like watercolors but more opaque and more gel-like than watercolor.
Encaustics A wax based paint like material that is mainly usead for impasto.
Sculpture Ceramics Usually made of clay, ceramics are things like bowls, plates or vases that are fired and covered in glaze
Statues Made out of clay, stone or plaster, statues are sculptures that are of 3d objects. Most commonly, people.
Plaster A medium that comes in a powder form and mixed with water and let to harden. It is like concrete but less stable and strong.
Abstract Usually made of random things like trash, plastic or knick knacks it is a compiled sculpture with no true figure or form.
Paper Usually paper mache, or carved books, paper sculptures are very complicated, time consuming and mathmatical.
Digital Animation A type of art that frame by frame creates the illusion of the picture moving or in motion.
Graphic Design A type of digital art focused on design of logos, lettering and more.
3D Art Art that is compiled on a 3D model digitally or 3D printed
2D Art Usually the use of a drawing tablet is used for this but, it is just like drawing on paper but instead is digitally done.
Pixel Using individual pixles to create a work of art
Mathmatical Using math in art to form precise, strategic and sometimes mirrored works of art.
Extras Multi Media A form of art that uses 2 or more mediums to compile one singular piece of art
Photography Taken with a camara, photography is considered a form of art. Using photagraphy as a source to express yourself much like traditionl artists.
