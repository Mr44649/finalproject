Origins

Digital art began in the 1970s when computers were becoming more advanced and people started to develop more uses for computers. One being digital art.

Types

There are many types of digital art. Animation is one of the main ones and you can learn more about that on our "types of art " tab. A few other big ones are graphic design, web design, 3D and 2D art, mathmatical art and AI generated art. 3D printing can also be considered a form of digital art.

What It Is

Digital art is a form of art that is not physical and is generated via a computer. It can be done with a drawing tablet or by having the computer itself generate art or manipulate a picture.
